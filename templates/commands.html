How It Works

This Discord bot is built with discord.py and organizes its functionality into three primary areas:

  1. Administration: Commands like /ban, /purge, and credit management (/addc, /removec, /gac) require Administrator permission.
  2. NSFW Content: Search adult video sources (/search, /jav_search), browse sites (/sites), and fetch videos/models.
  3. Utility & Credits: Claim daily credits (/daily), check your balance (/credits), and see top users (/leaderboard).

Use /help any time for detailed usage. Jail commands (/setupjail + /jail) let you moderate members by moving them in and out of a ‚Äújail‚Äù role.
